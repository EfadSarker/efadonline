About

Marketer by training, storyteller by instinct.

I'm a Marketing major with a minor in International Business at Independent University, Bangladesh (IUB), currently gaining hands-on industry experience as a Digital Marketing Intern at Nagad. Before that, I spent years behind the scenes as a Multimedia Journalist at SportsZone24, producing and editing video, photo, and voiceover content for digital and broadcast platforms.

That newsroom background shapes how I approach marketing: I care about clear, accurate, audience-first communication as much as I care about strategy and brand positioning. I'm equally comfortable researching a market, scripting a story, or managing a social calendar.

Outside of coursework and internships, I stay active in university life as a member of the IUB E-Business Club, and earned recognition as Best Delegate at the Dhaka University National Model United Nations — proof that I enjoy environments that reward preparation, negotiation, and clear thinking under pressure.

  • Goal: grow into a strategic digital marketing role that blends brand, content, and data.
  • Strength: turning research and raw information into clear, engaging content.
  • Passion: storytelling that sits at the intersection of media and marketing.
